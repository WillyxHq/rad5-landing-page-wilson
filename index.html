We help entrepreneurs and innovators build new in-house ventures and accelerate innovation.

At RAD5 Tech Hub, we work closely with our early-stage entrepreneurs to get their ideas into the market, create product-market fit, and scale fast.

Our Services

Venture Building

As a venture studio, we invest in potential entrepreneurs and innovators to build new ventures and accelerate within the firm. We embed our team of top-tier operators into the ventures to work side-by-side with the entrepreneurs for a higher probability of success.

Software Projects

We help individuals and organizations to build and deliver technology projects ranging from Web Apps to Mobile Apps, and Database Management. We leverage our 4Ps Approach to help our new and existing clients to out stand from their competition.

Our Social Projects

We have a corporate responsibility to drive the adoption of technology in under-served locations. We achieve this through collaboration and partnerships with different organizations. We promote the TeenHack, Abia4Tech and Girls who Code Initiatives.
